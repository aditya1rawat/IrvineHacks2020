In today's society, everyone on the planet is in constant need of transportation. From cars to trucks to buses. We never consider, however, what impact it has on our environment. Something we often forget is the fact that our planet is reporting record high temperatures and that fires are all over the world. We refuse to accept change, but that change is required if we are to improve the condition of the planet and to preserve the fossil fuels we all so desperately require. Our organization is made to show you your own effects on the planet whenever you use your form of personal temperature. Together, we can change the rising temperatures and get the planet to cool off.
